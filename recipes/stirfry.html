Chicken Stir Fry

Recpie (makes 2 portions)

Ingredients

  • Bag of fresh noodles for 2
  • Bag of stir fry veg
  • Packet of supermarket stir fry sauce of your choosing
  • Packet of 2 fresh chicken breast

Let's do it:

  1. Dice the chicken breast and fry in a wok until cooked through
  2. Add the stir fry veg for 4-5 minutes
  3. Add the noodles - after one minute add the packet of sauce.
  4. After another minute and a half take everything off the heat.
Enjoy!
