Spaghetti Bolognese

Return home

Recipe (makes five portions)

Ingredients

  • 500g lean mince beef
  • Big bag of spinach
  • Two cans chopped tomatoes
  • One oxo cube
  • Tomato puree
  • Three white onions
  • Two cloves garlic
  • 500g dried pasta

So let's begin:

  1. Chop the onions and mince the garlic
  2. Fry the garlic for 6-8 minutes on a medium heat - add the minced garlic about five minutes in
  3. Drop in the minced beef and cook until brown all over
  4. While this is cooking, quickly chop your bag of spinach (which you should wash first)
  5. Add your spinach when meat is down and reduce
  6. Add your cans of chopped tomatoes and reduce to a simmer
  7. Add a splash of beef stock and simmer
  8. Cook your dried pasta for c.10 minutes
  9. The timings should align - take everything off the heat and enjoy
