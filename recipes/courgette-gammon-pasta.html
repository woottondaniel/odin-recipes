Courgette & Gammon Pasta

Recipe (makes 4-5 portions)

Ingredients

  • Two smoked gammon steaks
  • Three red onions
  • Three courgettes
  • 500g dried farfalle pasta
  • Dried parsley
  • Olive oil
  • 750g tub of creme fraiche
  • Rock salt

Let's do it:

  1. Chop the courgettes into medium-thin slices and dice the onions
  2. Chop up the gammon steaks into small cubes aprox. 2.5cm by 2.5cm
  3. In one frying pan, put the onions and fry in olive oil on medium heat until the colour begins to change
  4. Then put the courgettes in with the onions and add some salt, meanwhile, bring a pot of salted water to the boil
  5. Fry the gammon in a separate frying pan until the fat boils off, add some salt and pepper
  6. In the meantime, add the pasta to the water for c.10 minutes, and keep stirring the courgettes and onions until the courgettes begin to shrink and slightly bronze
  7. Drain the pasta when cooked
  8. Take everything off the heat and combine in one pot
  9. Add the creme fraiche, a large sprinkle of parsley, and stir

Enjoy!
