Chicken Stir Fry (cooks 2)

Ingredients

    Bag of fresh noodles for 2 Bag of stir fry veg Packet of supermarket stir fry sauce of your choosing Packet of 2 fresh chicken breast

Let's do it:

    Dice the chicken breast and fry in a wok until cooked through Add the stir fry veg for 4-5 minutes Add the noodles - after one minute add the packet of sauce. After another minute and a half take everything off the heat.

Enjoy!
